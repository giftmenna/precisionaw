Copyright Infringement Policy

Last Updated: March, 2025

If you believe any content hosted on precisionaw.org infringes copyright, 
you may submit a formal notification containing all relevant information.

Copyright Notification Guidelines

Precision Academic World respects intellectual property rights and will respond to all valid 
copyright infringement notifications per applicable regulations. Upon receiving a compliant 
notification, we may:

- Remove or disable access to allegedly infringing material
- Take appropriate action including account termination
- Notify the content provider for their response

Notification Requirements

Your copyright infringement notification must include:

• Physical or electronic signature of rights holder/agent
• Identification of copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
• Identification of infringing material with specific URL(s)
• Your contact information (address, phone, email)
• Statement of good faith belief about unauthorized use
• Statement that information provided is accurate

Submit notifications to:
copyright@precisionaw.org

Content Provider Rights

Users who posted allegedly infringing material may submit:
- Counter-notification with valid justification
- Proof of ownership/license
- Contact information for verification

We recommend seeking legal advice if uncertain about infringement claims.

Repeat Offenders

Accounts with multiple valid infringement claims may be:
- Permanently suspended
- Barred from future competitions
- Subject to legal action

Reporting Abuse

We maintain zero tolerance for abusive content. Report concerns to:
abuse@precisionaw.org

Include in your report:
- Detailed description of issue
- Relevant URLs/timestamps
- Supporting evidence
